Freaquently Asked Question

1. Event Details (Time Location)

The event will be held on Saturday, October 22 9:00 am – 9:00 pm in Clough Undergraduate Learning Center (CULC) – Room 152.

2. What is the contest structure?

This will be a BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) Event for teams participating. You will be competing against teams from other Atlanta universities, so feel free to represent your school by wearing your school colors! We will have links with public data sets available to you. We highly encourage teams to strategize finding their own data to run analytics to reach a conclusion.

3. What should I bring?

Please bring a computer, headphones, and anything else you might need. We will be providing food so do not worry about bringing food. Please notify us if you have any dietary restrictions.

4. Who is eligible?

Anyone with skills in data science and who is 18+ years of age. Teams can be up to eight members and can be registered now or online, up until the Friday, October 21.

5. What is Datathon?

Datathon is the Georgia Tech Undergraduate Consulting Club official hackathon. This is not only an opportunity for students to push their data science skills to the limit, but also to make connections that will last you a life time.

6. How much does it cost?

Nothing, but what you will gain is everything.

7. Can I use past projects?

Please leave your masterpieces at home. However, you are free to use open source libraries as well as existing platforms and services.
